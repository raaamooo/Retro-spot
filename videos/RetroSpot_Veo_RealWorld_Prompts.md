## REAL LOCATION DESCRIPTION (EXTRACTED FROM FOOTAGE)

```
LOCATION: Retro Spot café and workspace, located on a narrow residential street
in Egypt. The street is paved, flanked by mid-rise concrete apartment buildings.
Trees line the left side. A car is parked further up the street.

EXTERIOR: White awning with deep burgundy serif typography reading "RETRO SPOT".
Below the awning: ornate arabesque/damask pattern printed on the fascia.
The entrance is a tall, arched black wooden door with glass panels — warm
amber light glows from inside. Outdoor seating uses black mesh café chairs
with teal cushions arranged on a raised white-brick platform.

ART WALL: To the right of the entrance hangs a large hand-printed tapestry/rug
depicting a classical Roman-style profile portrait in high-contrast black and white,
framed by an ornate floral rug in burgundy and gold. This is the dominant visual
landmark of the exterior.

STREET MURAL: Down the same street, on a neighboring building's deep red wall,
a large classical female figure mural is painted — a woman in a draped green
robe, rendered in a painterly academic style. Bicycles are parked in front.

INTERIOR (glimpsed through glass): Warm amber lighting, plush seating, framed
paintings on the walls, a sense of salon-like cultural depth.
```

## GLOBAL STYLE REFERENCE

```
STYLE: Cinematic. 9:16 vertical aspect ratio (Instagram Reels). 24fps.
Lens: 35mm prime, shallow depth of field, slight grain.
COLOR TREATMENT: Start in desaturated/monochrome world.
Retro Spot itself — the signage, awning, interior glow, art pieces —
erupts in full warm color: deep burgundy, warm amber, ivory, and ochre.
The surrounding street remains largely desaturated/muted.
Audio: vinyl crackle + ambient street sounds transitioning into soft jazz
as the camera enters or approaches Retro Spot.
On-screen text: aged cream serif vintage typography.
```

### SHOT 1 — Wide street, slow push toward Retro Spot

```
REFERENCE IMAGES: screenshots/painting_01.jpg, screenshots/painting_02.jpg, screenshots/painting_03.jpg

Wide shot, eye-level, from the far end of the street looking toward Retro Spot.
Narrow residential Egyptian street, concrete buildings both sides.
Street is entirely desaturated — grey pavement, grey facades, overcast hazy sky.
On the left side, roughly mid-frame: the Retro Spot storefront begins to appear.
The deep burgundy "Retro Spot" hanging sign is the first saturated element — it
pulses with warm color while everything else stays grey.

```

The mural color glows — warm green and ochre, isolated against the grey world.
8 seconds.
